Tusker Group is a leading Security and Facility Management Company with its corporate office in Gurgaon. As a group our focus lies in designing services which makes a visibly difference for our clients and to their businesses. We tailor our services to look at the smallest details that can create a great customer experience.

We are supported by our talented team and backed up by leading-edge technology along with huge experience in providing services in Facility & Property Management, Security Services, Detective & Investigation Services, Electronic Security, Manpower outsourcing and other related services. Tusker has a unique approach that builds long lasting relationships with our customers based on our expertise and values.

Tusker Group was founded by Major R.K. Rao (Veteran) in 2008 after completion of 29 years of glorious services in Indian Army. Due to his inclination and continuous association after retirement, he established his own group name i.e TUSKER which means the Elephant. Our unique ability is to provide the best services at competitive cost. Tusker has become the known and trustworthy brand in the security and facility managegment industry.
